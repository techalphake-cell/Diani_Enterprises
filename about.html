Our Story

Built on the South Coast

In 2014, Diani was rapidly growing, its stunning coastline attracting world-class developers, hoteliers, and homeowners. Seeing the unique challenges of building and maintaining premium properties in a coastal environment, Mr. Sita Ram Saini founded Diani Enterprises. What started as a modest local shop has grown over the last 12 years into Diani’s premier hardware partner. Mr. Saini established the business on a simple, powerful promise: to provide the highest quality materials, reliable service, and deep technical expertise to the hands building our coastal paradise.

The coastal climate of Diani requires specialized building solutions. From combating saltwater corrosion to maintaining the pristine aesthetic of luxury properties, local businesses need more than just standard hardware—they need tailored solutions. Over the past decade, Diani Enterprises has become the trusted backbone for: Luxury Hotels & Resorts: Providing heavy-duty, commercial-grade plumbing, electrical, and maintenance supplies to keep guest experiences seamless. Private Villas & Cottages: Supplying high-finish fixtures, premium paints, and architectural hardware that elevate coastal living spaces. Local Businesses & Contractors: Offering a reliable bulk inventory of construction essentials, timber, and specialty tools to keep local projects on time and on budget. "We don’t just sell hardware; we supply the peace of mind that keeps Diani’s tourism and business sectors thriving." — Mr. Sita Ram Saini, Founder

As Diani continues to evolve, Diani Enterprises remains committed to innovation, sustainability, and community. We are incredibly grateful to the hotels, villas, builders, and neighbors who have trusted us with their projects for the last 12 years. Whether you are breaking ground on a new beachfront property or upgrading an established boutique hotel, Diani Enterprises is here to ensure your vision stands strong for years to come.
